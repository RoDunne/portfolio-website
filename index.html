    About Me

    Hi there, I'm Robert, welcome to my section of the internet. So, picture this: after high school, I stumbled into custodial work. College? Nah, couldn't afford it. I had to get a job first. Life happened, and suddenly, custodial wasn't just a pit stop; it became the main gig.

    But hold up, plot twist! When the chance came knocking, I moved back in with the folks, rocked my college degree, and snagged some certificates along the way. Now, you might glance at my resume and see "custodian extraordinaire," but let me tell you, that's just one chapter. I'm all about learning; I don't hit the brakes. Sure, my work experience might look boring, but my attitude screams "relentless learner." Give me a challenge, and I'll meet it head-on until I'm the best in class.

    Beyond the career, I'm all about nature - fishing, camping, off-roading, you name it. Video games and board games? Sign me up. I'm always down for sightseeing and the joy of devouring some good food. And speaking of food, I love to cook up a big feast.

    Now, let's get real. I'm not just looking for a job; I want a cause to champion. Consider me not just a professional but a man on a mission. Thanks for having a look into my world, and I hope you see the spark that makes me more than just a resume. Cheers!
